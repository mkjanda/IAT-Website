My software allows you to design your own IAT with your own images and text and, if you choose to use them, numerous options. Once you've finished designing your test, you upload it to my server for administration. Then you download the results when you're ready to examine them. With a bit of programming knowledge, it's possible to retrieve results on-the-fly as opposed to downloading all results and exporting them to an Excel file. If you're ready to test it already, click on the Get the Software button above.

Though the software only supports the 7-Block IAT format developed by Greenwald, it allows you to customize the layout of your IAT, choose either words or images in your response keys, and create instruction blocks to precede any or all sections of the test. It also allows you to design questionnaires to precede and follow your IAT. For the more advanced user, it is possible to link IAT results with data gathered on other web sites.
