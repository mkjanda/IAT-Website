An Implicit Association Test is a means of measuring attitudes involving bias in individuals in a non-falsifiable manner. Non-falsifiable how? Test items are typically responded to in a handful of seconds and the response time is recorded. Methods have been devised for processing these response times, or latencies, to yield a meaningful value.

Why does this site exist? Because I've developed software that allows people to design IATs with a point and click interface and administer them on the Internet.
